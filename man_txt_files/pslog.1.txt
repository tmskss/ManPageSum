## NAME         top
           pslog - report current logs path of a process
## SYNOPSIS         top
           pslog pid ...
           pslog -V
## DESCRIPTION         top
           The pslog command reports the current working logs of a process.
## OPTIONS         top
           -V     Display version information.
## SEE ALSO         top
           pgrep(1), ps(1), pwdx(1).
